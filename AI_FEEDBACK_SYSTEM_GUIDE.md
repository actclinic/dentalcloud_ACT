# AI Assistant Feedback System Guide

## Overview
The Dental Cloud AI Assistant (Loli) now includes a feedback system that allows you to rate responses as helpful or not helpful. This helps the AI learn and improve over time.

## How to Use the Feedback System

### Rating Responses
1. After the AI assistant responds to your question, you'll see two buttons below the response:
   - 👍 **Thumbs Up**: Click this if the response was helpful
   - 👎 **Thumbs Down**: Click this if the response was not helpful

2. The button will change color to show your selection:
   - Green: Your response was marked as helpful
   - Red: Your response was marked as not helpful
   - Gray: No rating given yet

### When to Give Feedback
- **Rate as Helpful (👍)** when:
  - The AI provided accurate information
  - The response solved your problem
  - The suggestion was practical and useful
  - The response was clear and well-formatted

- **Rate as Not Helpful (👎)** when:
  - The information was incorrect
  - The response didn't address your question
  - The suggestion wasn't practical for your situation
  - The response was unclear or confusing

## Benefits of Using the Feedback System

### For You:
- The AI will learn to provide responses that better match your needs
- Over time, you'll receive more relevant and helpful answers
- The AI will adapt its communication style based on your preferences

### For the Team:
- Helps improve the AI assistant for everyone
- Makes the system more accurate and useful
- Contributes to better patient care through improved AI support

## Tips for Effective Feedback
- Rate responses regularly to help the AI learn faster
- Be consistent with your ratings
- Even small interactions help train the AI
- The system remembers patterns in your feedback to personalize responses

## Privacy Note
- Your feedback is stored securely in the application
- Individual ratings are anonymous
- Only aggregate patterns are used to improve AI responses
- No personal information is shared outside the system

## Questions?
If you have questions about using the feedback system:
- Look for the Quick Help button in the AI assistant
- Refer to the main help guide
- Ask a team leader for assistance
