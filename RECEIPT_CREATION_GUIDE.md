# How to Create Patient Receipts in Dental Cloud

## Simple Guide for Nurses

### What is a Receipt?
A receipt in our system is a record showing that a patient has made a payment for dental services. It shows what they paid for and how much money was collected.

### When Do You Need to Create a Receipt?
- When a patient pays for a dental procedure on the same day
- When a patient pays toward their outstanding balance
- When a patient makes a partial payment
- Any time money is collected from a patient

### Step-by-Step Process

#### Step 1: Prepare Patient Information
Before creating a receipt, make sure you have:
- The patient's name (you can use just their name, no need for ID numbers)
- The amount they are paying
- Information about what they're paying for (treatment received or balance payment)

#### Step 2: Use the AI Assistant to Create the Receipt
1. Open the AI Assistant (Loli) in the Dental Cloud app
2. Make sure you're in **Agent Mode** (the purple button) - this is important for processing payments
3. Tell Loli something like:
   - "Process a payment of 15000 MMK from John Smith"
   - "Create a receipt for Mary Johnson who paid 25000 MMK"
   - "Record payment from Sarah Davis, amount 18000 MMK"

#### Step 3: Provide Additional Details (if needed)
The AI might ask for more information:
- If it's for a specific treatment
- If it's paying off an existing balance
- Which services were provided

### Common Scenarios

#### Scenario 1: Payment for Today's Treatment
Patient received a filling and is paying for it immediately:
1. Tell the AI: "I completed a filling on tooth #18 for John Smith, cost 25000 MMK, and he wants to pay now"
2. The system will record both the treatment and the payment

#### Scenario 2: Payment for Outstanding Balance
Patient is paying off an old bill:
1. Tell the AI: "John Smith is paying 50000 MMK toward his outstanding balance"
2. The system will reduce their balance and create a receipt

#### Scenario 3: Partial Payment
Patient can't pay the full amount but wants to make a partial payment:
1. Tell the AI: "Mary Johnson is making a partial payment of 30000 MMK on her 80000 MMK balance"
2. The system will update their remaining balance and create a receipt

### Important Tips
- Always confirm the patient's name spelling with them
- Double-check the payment amount before submitting
- Make sure you're in Agent Mode when processing payments
- The receipt is automatically saved in the system
- Patient's balance will update automatically after payment

### What Happens After You Create a Receipt?
- The patient's outstanding balance decreases
- The payment is recorded in the financial system
- A receipt record is created that can be printed or viewed later
- The payment contributes to the day's revenue

### Troubleshooting
**Problem:** AI says "Agent Mode Required"
**Solution:** Click the purple "Agent Mode" button before trying again

**Problem:** AI can't find the patient
**Solution:** Check the patient's name spelling, or ask them for their registered name

**Problem:** Payment didn't process
**Solution:** Make sure you provided both the patient name and exact payment amount

### Questions to Ask the AI Assistant
- "Process payment of [amount] from [patient name]"
- "Create receipt for [patient name] who paid [amount]"
- "How do I record a payment for [patient name]?"
- "Show me [patient name]'s current balance before I process payment"

Remember: The AI Assistant (Loli) is designed to help you with these tasks using simple, everyday language. You don't need to use technical terms or codes!
