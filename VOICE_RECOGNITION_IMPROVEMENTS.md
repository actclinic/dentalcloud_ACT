## Troubleshooting

### If Recognition Fails:
- Check microphone permissions in browser settings
- Ensure you're in a quiet environment
- Speak clearly and at normal volume
- Try rephrasing if initial recognition is poor

### Common Issues:
- **"No speech detected"**: Speak louder or check microphone connection
- **"Microphone access denied"**: Allow microphone permissions in browser
- **"Network error"**: Check internet connection
- **Poor accuracy**: Try speaking more clearly or using simpler terms
